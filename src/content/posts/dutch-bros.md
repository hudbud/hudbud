Dutch Bros is a coffee chain in the western USA which approached Hathway with the goal of improving their loyalty program. I helped bring this project from Sketch to Figma, converting all symbols into components, creating new global type and color styles, and ensuring proper architecture for each component. I created a new, clearer layout for the design system and continue to manage it as needed.

**Roles:** Design Systems Management, UI Support, Graphic Design

**Tools:** Figma, Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator

**Agency:** Hathway
