Blaze Pizza approached Hathway to create an ordering and loyalty app and web experience. I took over the project and designed a visual refresh for the UI, transforming the previous white-label design into a more branded experience featuring bold black buttons and vibrant imagery. I collaborated directly with the client on design presentations and worked with developers, managing the UI toolkit and design system before converting the project to Figma.

**Roles:** UI Design, UI Toolkit Management, Art Direction, Figma Conversion

**Tools:** Sketch, Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop

**Agency:** Hathway
