Pieology is a QSR pizza chain that commissioned Hathway to develop an ordering and loyalty app plus web ordering platform. I contributed by designing and maintaining the design system, creating UI screens, and developing hand-drawn icons for the site and app. The approach emphasized clean design while expressing the brand's "hand-made" ethos through custom iconography and illustrations.

**Roles:** UI Support, UI Toolkit Support, Hand-drawn Iconography

**Tools:** Sketch, Illustrator, Procreate, Figma

**Agency:** Hathway
