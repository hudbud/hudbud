Red Robin sought to enhance their online ordering experience. The team developed a mobile ordering app, mobile ordering website, and desktop ordering website. The design emphasized whitespace and efficient UX to reduce bounce rates and optimize conversion paths, creating a visually appealing and functional ordering system.

**Roles:** UI Design, Design Systems Management

**Tools:** Figma, Photoshop
