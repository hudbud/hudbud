Pantepic is a map-based secure messenger that successfully launched on iOS and Android, exceeding 1,000 users in its first 3 months. I designed the logo and full branding, UI for the app (including scheduled messages, self-destruct, and SOS features), Mapbox map styling, marketing collateral, web design, and sticker design.

**Roles:** Logo & Branding, UI Design, Mapbox Design, Marketing Collateral, Web Design, Sticker Design

**Tools:** Figma, Illustrator, Mapbox
